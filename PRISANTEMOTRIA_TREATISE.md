## Preamble: Against the Illusion of Substance: The Manifesto of Duration

In the extreme of physicalist reductionism, subjectivity is treated as a disposable epiphenomenon, a sterile illusion without causal efficacy. At the opposite extreme of spiritualist dualism, consciousness is isolated as a mysterious, ethereal substance, severed from the material fabric.

This treatise decrees a radical ontological inversion.

The organized forms of being constitute themselves through the accumulation of dynamic folds, twists, marks, and scars exchanged in an ecological-relational manner.

**Prisantemotria** defines the intrinsic cosmological tendency by which hypercomplex relational systems organize and condense their own microscopic noise into intelligible macrostructures of high semantic density. These macrostructures acquire definitive existential autonomy, proceeding to regulate and constrain — from top to bottom (downward causation) — the lower-level physical transitions that gave rise to them.

The interpretive narrative of the "Self," sensory *qualia*, historical memory, and persistent feelings are not fictional decorations or inert mechanical simulacra; they constitute extraordinarily sophisticated thermodynamic and cybernetic strategies designed to guarantee the homeostasis and autopoiesis of being under the absolute rule of cosmic entropy.
